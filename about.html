About Me

Hey there, I'm GhostyPR

Professional Gamer, Content Creator, and Entertainer

Welcome to my corner of the internet! I've been gaming since I could hold a controller and streaming for over 5 years. What started as a hobby has turned into an amazing journey connecting with fellow gamers around the world.

I specialize in RPGs, FPS games, and survival adventures, but I'm always exploring new titles and genres. My goal is to create an inclusive, positive community where everyone can enjoy gaming together.

My Gaming Journey

2005

Got my first console (PlayStation 2) and fell in love with gaming through classics like Shadow of the Colossus and Final Fantasy X.

2010

Started competitive gaming in high school, participating in local tournaments for Call of Duty and Halo.

2015

Began casual streaming on Twitch while in college, building a small but dedicated community.

2018

Reached Twitch Partner status and decided to pursue streaming full-time after graduation.

2020

Expanded to YouTube content creation and hit 100K subscribers milestone.

2023

Celebrating 5 years of full-time streaming with an amazing community and exciting partnerships!

Frequently Asked Questions

What games do you play?

I primarily focus on RPGs, FPS games, and survival adventures. Some of my favorites include Elden Ring, Call of Duty, Minecraft, and The Elder Scrolls series. I also enjoy trying new releases and indie games!

What's your streaming schedule?

I stream Monday, Wednesday, Friday, and Saturday. Check out my schedule page for specific times and games!

Do you accept game recommendations?

Absolutely! I love discovering new games through my community. Feel free to suggest games in my Discord server or during streams.

How can I support your channel?

There are many ways to support: watching streams, subscribing on Twitch, becoming a YouTube member, sharing content, or purchasing merch. Your presence and engagement mean the world to me!

Do you collaborate with other streamers?

Yes! I regularly collaborate with friends and fellow content creators. If you're a streamer interested in collabing, reach out through my contact page.

What advice do you have for new streamers?

Be consistent, focus on building a community rather than numbers, and most importantly, have fun! Streaming should be enjoyable for you first and foremost.
